Broccoli and Pork

Description

In this dish, we'll be using pork marinated(optional) in soy sauce and sugar to make a simple and easy meal. We'll begin by putting a light layer of cooking oil in a wok before putting in cut pieces of pork slices. Once they're half way cooked, we'll add broccoli, some water, and a little corn starch mixed with water at the end.

Ingredients

  • pork
  • broccoli
  • soy sauce
  • corn starch
  • cooking oil
  • salt

Steps

  1. Add cooking oil to pot till it becomes steamy
  2. Put in slices of pork
  3. Wait till pork is half-way cooked
  4. Add broccoli and a half cup of water
  5. Add a thin layer of salt and stir
  6. When broccoli and pork are almost finished, add quarter cup of corn starch mixed with water to wok
  7. Mix and ready to serve!
