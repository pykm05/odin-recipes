Spaghetti and Meatballs

back to homepage

Description

We'll be making some simple spaghetti and meatball in this recipe using store-bought tomato sauce and spaghetti noodles. To start, boil some water in a wok before adding the spaghetti. Wait till the spaghetti is cooked nicely before dumping the water out and adding totmato sauce and meatballs. It's that simple!

Ingredients

  • spaghetti
  • tomato sauce
  • meatballs
  • salt

Steps

  1. Boil water in pot/wok and drizzle a bit of salt
  2. When water is boiling, add noodles and stir
  3. Wait till noodles are cooked and pour water out
  4. Add tomato sauce and meatballs to pot/wok
  5. Mix it and ready to serve once meatballs are cooked!
